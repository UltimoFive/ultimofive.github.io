About Me

I grew up in the small town of Westfield New York, where after graduation from High School I attended Jamestown Community College to obtain my Associates in Information Technology. After a few years of realizing that I.T. isn't really for me, I spent some time becoming a decently successful YouTube and Twitch partner where I created guides and gaming videos. During this time, I started working towards a career of being a store manager at GameStop, but after some time I realized I wanted more.

I wanted to accomplish something that could help me start a family and feel more fulfilling than just being a store manager, so I left GameStop to focus more on college to earn my bachelor's degree. Throughout my life I grew up around technology and because of this I am able to adapt quickly to new technology, software, and game development platforms. With this knack I decided to attend Southern New Hampshire University Online where simultaneously where I attended Full Time as I worked towards my chosen degree of Game Development and Programming.

Of course, throughout my time in college I had changed my mind between Game Development and Programming and Computer Science. However, I found a love for Game Development where I am able to use my creativity to explore new and different ways to create immersive worlds that tell stories. For instance, I currently have 5 projects with one, called Project Cyber, that I am working on as of this moment with a small team of people. Alongside this I also came up with a 2D Sidescroller about a dream world, a roguelike FPS similar to Gunfire Reborn inspired by DnD, and a Survival series from a strange nightmare that I had.

I feel that due to my idea creation, multitasking ability, hard work ethic, and management background I am a great consideration for being part of a development team. I have discovered that I don't want to just be a gamer, and I want to create amazing worlds with others that make people enjoy their time and be happy. Of course I still feel wonderful and fulfilled just in general as a software engineer or game developer, because I just love to program and I can't wait to see where it takes me.
